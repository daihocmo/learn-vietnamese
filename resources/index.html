    Resources

    Welcome to the Resources Sheet, resources are categorised into "Learning" Vietnamese and "Acquiring" Vietnamese.

    Learning Vietnamese

    Alphabet

    • Flexiclasses Vietnamese Learning Blog: All about Vietnamese alphabet, learning how to read and write here.
    • Vietnamese Alphabet by MyLanguages.org: Yet another Vietnamese alphabet guide.

    Vocabulary

    • Vietnamese Vocabulary Core 1k by Soi: Vietnamese Core 1K Vocab Anki Deck made by Soi
    • Xefjord's Complete Vietnamese - Northern Dialect: The Vietnamese Anki deck from this project.
    • Vietnamese Vocabulary Lists: A complete list of basic Vietnamese vocabulary made by VietnamesePod101.

    Grammar

    • onlyduyy's guide to Vietnamese Grammar: Yet another Vietnamese grammar guide that doesn't suck. Currently in progress.

    • Learning Vietnamese Grammar with TVO: A grammar series in Northern dialect made by Tieng Viet Oi, despite being a Vietnamese learning channel there are many videos made for native speakers.

    • Elementary Vietnamese by Binh Nhu Ngo: Excellent reviews from community, I haven't read it myself, you can buy the book directly on Amazon (2003 Revised Version).

    • seahorse viet grammar's archive: Many grammar points but without sorting.

    • Vietnamese Grammar by Soi: An Anki deck for learning Vietnamese Grammar. There are audios for different dialects!

    Chữ Nôm

    • ChuNom.org: There are some Chữ Nôm course offered by ChuNom.org
    • Nom Foundation: an American nonprofit agency for Han Nom preservation.

    Acquire Vietnamese

    Movies & TV Shows

    You just have to click one of the links here and it will directs you to the Vietnamese movie collection (Instead of the website's main page).

    • FPTPlay: Watching Vietnamese movies for free, you just have to use your Google or Facebook account.
    • TV360: Same as above.
    • VieChannel: There are a lot of gameshows in this channel.

    Novels and Literary Texts

    Real Vietnamese literature

    • Văn học trẻ - Truyện ngắn: Collection of short stories.
    • Báo Nhân Dân - Mục truyện ngắn: Collection of short stories from Nhan Dan Newspaper.
    • Hội nhà văn - Truyện ngắn: Same as above, but written by people from "Hội nhà văn".
    • Wikisource Vietnamese Short Stories Collection: Collection of short stories from Wikisource
    • 1000 cuốn sách vang bóng một thời: old Vietnamese books (expired copyright) being digitalized.

    Light novel, web novel

    • Hako: Light novels and Web novels translated to Vietnamese
    • DTV-Ebook: Download various Romance ebooks.

    Manga

    • Manga Recommendation: From Japanese Media Recommendation, you can choose the same manga to read on any of these manga reading sites.
    • Blogtruyenmoi: A manga reader site made by Ngankvn, most of the mangas here are uploaded by users or scanlated groups.
    • Nettruyen: A pretty large manga reader site.
    • TruyenQQVN: Another manga (and manhwa, manhua) reading site.

    Audiobooks & Podcasts

    • Vietnamese Podcast Suggestion: A list of suggestions for which Vietnamese podcasts to listen to.
    • TVO Podcast: A Podcast made by TiengVietOi - A Vietnamese learning Youtube channel.
    • Podcastindex: Listen to Podcast online without sign-in, not just Vietnamese Podcasts.

    Forums

    • VOZ: VOZ (other names: Vnoczone, vOz, vOzforums, Vietnam Overclockers Zone) an online forum has been established since 2001, one of the largest Technogoly forum in Vietnamese. Despite the description, this forum has a lot of things to offer, not just Science & Technology.
    • OTOFUN: Primarily about cars and auto.
    • Blogtruyen Forum: An Forum for manga lovers, you can also read Vietnamese-translated manga here.
    • TVE-4U: The forum for book lovers and for downloading Vietnamese books made by volunteers.

    News

    • VTVNews: Daily news about Vietnam.
    • Vietnamnet: News
    • Dân trí
    • VNExpress
    • Tạp chí Tia sáng: A Vietnamese science & technology magazine.

    Dictionaries

    Yomichan

    Yomichan, a browser add-on that allows you to look up Japanese (and Vietnamese) words easily.

    • VNEDICT by Paul Denisowski converted by Marsh Nguyễn for Yomichan.
    • Chữ Nôm Dictionary Chữ Nôm Dictionary converted by Marsh Nguyễn. The data comes from https://chunom.org/ The dictionary contains 1,569 entries.
    • Từ Điển Tiếng Việt Thông Dụng Vie-Vie dictionary converted by Marsh Nguyễn. The dictionary data is from Từ Điển Tiếng Việt Thông Dụng and was sourced from https://github.com/vntk/dictionary/tree/master/data contains 42012 entries.

    Online

    • Youglish Vietnamese: Youghlish but for Vietnamese.
    • Forvo: Search for a word and hear it pronounced by a native speaker.
    • VietnamesePod101 Dictionary - A Vietnamese Dictionary made by VietnamesePod101.
    • VDict (bilingual) - If you are a beginner, use this. Remember to change the search mode to "Viet - Anh".

    E-Reader Dictionaries

    • VNEDICT in stardict format

    Other Tools

    • ※ Anki (Windows, Mac and Linux) - Spaced repetition flashcard program. A must have. It is tricky to use at first. So please see the Mining section for guides on how to set it up (From the main guide).

    • Unikey: Vietnamese Keyboard.

    Applications (Mobile)

    Android

    • AnkiDroid - Port of Anki for Android devices.

    • AnkiConnect for Android - use AnkiConnect for 1 click card creation on Android.

    • Kiwi Browser - Chromium based browser with extension support. You can use Yomichan on this.

    iOS

    • AnkiMobile - $24.99 official port of Anki for iOS.

    Guides/Methods/Motivation

    • All Japanese All The Time - The reason why All Japanese All The Time, often abbreviated to AJATT, is still popular to this day, is because of the mindset building that it was able to achieve. The articles on that site can get you in the Japanese immersion spirit better than I or any other guide can.
    • AntiMoon - AntiMoon is a site made for English learners. It promotes learning through large amounts of input, also known as immersion. It has good articles on some of the practical methods used in immersion.
    • TheMoeWay: Immersion-based Japanese guide.
    • Donkuri Guide
    • YouTube & Netflix mining guide - Youtube and netflix mining guide with asbplayer (also works with zoro.to and amazon prime video)

    "Other"

    • Why You Still Don't Understand Your Target Language - Great video for getting motivated to immerse more.
